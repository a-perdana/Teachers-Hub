Portfolio — your official CPD record
This is where you submit proof of a teaching competency at a level. HQ reviews every submission before granting the level.
  1. Pick the competency + level (Awareness / Practitioner / Advanced / Lead) you want to claim
  2. Write a short story (≥80 chars) about what you actually did in class + attach a file if it helps (lesson plan, student work, observation feedback, photos)
  3. Track the status next to your submission: Pending waiting for review · Under Review HQ has opened it · Approved lands on your Learning Path · Revise & Resubmit HQ left a note
⏳ Don't submit on day 1. Do the real work first, then come back. Self-assessment ratings live on the Competency Framework page and are private — this page is for the official record.
