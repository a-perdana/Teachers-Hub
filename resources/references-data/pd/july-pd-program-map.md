# July PD Program Map — The Whole Arc

This is the planning view for the Eduversal Academic Board and trainers. It shows **what to deliver, to whom, and in what order** for the July term-start PD across partner schools. Use it to build each school's real schedule.

> **Important:** this is a *menu and a sequence*, not a fixed timetable. Each school is different. Pick the sessions a school needs, in the order below, and fit them to the school's available time (a single PD day, a week of mornings, or a mix of face-to-face and online).

---

## The three audiences

| Tier | Who | Hub |
|---|---|---|
| **Teachers** | Subject teachers + subject leaders | Teachers Hub |
| **Leaders** | Principals, Academic + Cambridge Coordinators, Foundation Reps | Academic Hub |
| **Specialists** | Eduversal Specialists, Coordinators, Directors (the trainers) | Central Hub |

## The recommended sequence

The order matters. Each session builds on the one before.

### Step 0 — Before the schools: prepare the trainers
**Audience: Specialists / Coordinators / Directors.** Run this *before* any school session.

1. **`specialists/train-the-trainer-guide.md`** — so every trainer delivers consistently across schools.
2. **`specialists/session-specialist-walkthrough-lens.md`** — so Specialists hold the same observation lens on visits.

> If the trainers are not aligned first, every school gets a different Eduversal. This step is the foundation.

### Step 1 — Everyone: the shared map
**Audience: teachers AND leaders (can be a joint opening session).**

3. Hand out and walk **`00-program-overview/three-rating-systems-at-a-glance.md`**. This resolves the single biggest confusion (KPI vs Appraisal vs Competency) before any deep session. 20–30 minutes. Do it first.

### Step 2 — Teachers: understand the systems that apply to you
**Audience: teachers.** Order reflects what a teacher meets first in the year.

4. **`teachers/session-appraisal-for-teachers.md`** (+ `onepager-how-appraisal-works.md`) — removes appraisal fear.
5. **`teachers/session-kpi-for-teachers.md`** (+ `onepager-how-kpi-works.md`) — turns KPI into a year plan.
6. **`teachers/session-competency-self-assessment.md`** (+ `workbook-competency-self-assessment.md`) — first honest self-assessment.
7. **`teachers/session-ai-in-the-classroom.md`** — safe, useful AI + the red lines.

### Step 3 — Leaders: run the systems + grow as a leader
**Audience: school leaders.** Two of these (calibration, coaching) protect the fairness of the teacher appraisal — prioritise them.

8. Hand out **`leaders/onepager-leadership-evaluation-map.md`** — the two-hats map.
9. **`leaders/session-observer-calibration-f2.md`** (+ `workbook-f2-calibration-exercise.md`) — ⭐ makes the 50%-weighted F2 score fair across schools. **High priority.**
10. **`leaders/session-coaching-glow-grow-go.md`** — ⭐ makes the post-observation conversation useful. Pairs with #9.
11. **`leaders/session-kpi-target-setting.md`** — set weights that signal priorities; agree (not assign) targets.
12. **`leaders/session-leadership-competency.md`** — leaders' own growth map.

## Priority guide (if time is short)

If a school can only do a few sessions in July, prioritise in this order:

1. **Three Rating Systems at a Glance** (everyone) — resolves the core confusion. Non-negotiable.
2. **F2 Observer Calibration** (leaders) — protects the 50%-weighted appraisal across schools.
3. **Coaching: Glow/Grow/Go** (leaders) — makes the appraisal conversation change practice.
4. **Appraisal for Teachers** (teachers) — removes fear, builds trust.
5. Everything else, as time allows.

---

## Delivery modes

Every session has a face-to-face and an online variant (see each guide + `_facilitator-conventions.md` §3). A typical July pattern:

- **Face-to-face** for the high-interaction sessions (calibration, coaching, target-setting role-plays) — they need live discussion and reconciliation.
- **Online** for the one-pager walkthroughs and self-assessment sessions — they work well with shared screens and breakout rooms.
- **Blended** where a school's leaders are face-to-face but some teachers join online.

---

## After July (the follow-up loop)

PD that ends in July does not change practice by December. Build in follow-up:

- **Appraisal + coaching** — the next observation checks the "Go" step from the feedback conversation.
- **Competency** — teachers and leaders return to their Learning Path each term to claim new levels with evidence.
- **Calibration** — Specialists and leaders re-calibrate before each appraisal season.
- **Exit-ticket line 3** — collect "still unsure about…" from every session and plan the next round of support from what it shows.
