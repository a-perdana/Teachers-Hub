Cambridge Teacher Standards 2019 — University of Cambridge Local Examinations Syndicate (UCLES). Five attributes: Confident, Responsible, Reflective, Innovative, Engaged. Primary source for all domain structures and CPD level descriptors. ↗ cambridge.org
Permendiknas No. 16 Tahun 2007 — Standar Kualifikasi Akademik dan Kompetensi Guru — Kementerian Pendidikan Nasional RI. Four core teacher competencies: Pedagogik, Kepribadian, Sosial, Profesional. Integrated into Professional Identity & Ethics and Collaboration domains. ↗ peraturan.bpk.go.id
Cambridge CPD Framework — Professional Development Levels — Cambridge Assessment International Education. Four CPD progression levels (Awareness → Practitioner → Advanced → Lead/Mentor) used as the level structure throughout this framework. ↗ cambridge-community.org.uk
