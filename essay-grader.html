Essay Grader

Review short-answer items where the student's response didn't match the author's expected text exactly. Award full / partial / no credit. Subject leaders + admins only.

Most chapter test answers grade themselves. But a short-answer response that's right in a different way — a synonym, a paraphrase, a different spelling — can't be matched automatically, so it's flagged for a human. Here you read each flagged answer and award full, partial, or no credit. The student's score updates as soon as you decide.

For: Subject Leaders (admins too) Only flagged short answers appear Re-scores the attempt on save Final step of the assessment flow
An empty list is good news — it means every answer auto-matched and nothing needs your judgement right now.
How this works: chapter test items of type short are auto-graded by exact (case-insensitive) match. If a student's answer doesn't match but is plausibly correct (synonym, abbreviation, paraphrase), the attempt is marked flagged and the score is recalculated here when you award credit.
