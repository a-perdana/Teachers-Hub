When a new student signs in to Students Hub and picks one of your classes, they land here as Pending. You check the name, then Approve (they get full access) or Reject (they go back to pick again). Nothing else happens until you approve — so clearing this list is step one before any test can run.

For: Subject Teachers & Subject Leaders 3 tabs: Pending · Active · Rejected Scoped to your school's classes First step of the assessment flow
A student you reject is not deleted — they simply return to the class picker and can choose the right class. If someone is missing, they may have picked a different teacher's class.
